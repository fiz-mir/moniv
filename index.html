Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Moniv and how it can enhance accessibility for visually impaired users.

How does moniv detect currency?

moniv uses advanced AI models, including YOLOv8, to quickly identify and classify different Malaysian banknotes and coins, providing instant recognition for users.

Can moniv be used offline?

Yes, moniv offers an offline mode that allows users to access its currency detection features without an internet connection, making it convenient for all environments.

Does Moniv provide auditory feedback for identified currency?

Absolutely. moniv includes an auditory feedback system that announces the denomination, enabling users to recognize currency hands-free.

How accurate is Moniv's currency detection?

moniv’s detection is highly accurate, thanks to its YOLOv8 model. The app is continuously updated to improve accuracy with various lighting and background conditions.

Is moniv accessible for visually impaired users?

Yes, moniv is specifically designed for visually impaired users. It includes features like haptic feedback, screen reader compatibility, and clear auditory cues.

Can moniv identify currencies from other countries?

Currently, moniv is focused on recognizing Malaysian currencies. Future updates may include support for other currencies based on demand.
